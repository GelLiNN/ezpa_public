OVERVIEW
 

What started up our interest in making Prior Authorization (PA) efficient and easy?  A study published by Health Affairs adequately showed that Nurses, RNs, and Clerical staff are spending roughly one-third of their work week dealing with this process.

 

This means that even today those people call insurance companies, fill out physical forms, and wait days to sometimes find out that a PA request may not even be necessary.

​

It's time to intervene.  More quality care can be delivered to patients if clinical staff can spend less time dealing with insurance companies directly.  Today, technology is much more improved than it was when this process was established.

​

Today, new tools like Machine Learning allow us to utilize all the information necessary to make the determination for you.  Authorizations are based on health plans, diagnostic codes, patient medical history, and rules set forth by the insurance company or health plan policy.

​

We can compile all of these data and provide your hospital or practice with a powerful recommendations service and request management service.  We can tell you whether or not the patient is authorized right here, right now.
